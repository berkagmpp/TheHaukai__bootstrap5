PRIVACY POLICY

We collect personal information from you, including information about your:

• name
• contact information
• Location
• interactions with us



We collect your personal information in order to:

• reserve and book tables for you at The Haukai Restaurant
• prevent unauthorised access
• conduct our business and market our services



We keep your information safe by storing it in encrypted files and allowing only authorised staff to use it.


We keep your information for two years at which point we securely destroy it by erasing digital traces and manual documents are shredded.


You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong. If you’d like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at admin@thehaukai.nz, or 09-401-4019, or 1 Island Street, Kerikeri, Bay of Islands, New Zealand.
